======================
VISUAL ELEMENT DESCRIPTIONS
======================

These visual entries describe how common cinematic elements *look and feel*.  
They are used to make AI image prompts realistic, detailed, and faithful.

-------------------------
SETTINGS
-------------------------
tunnel: A long round metallic passage, dimly lit, with curved reflective walls and flickering orange lights.
forest: Dense green trees, foggy atmosphere, light beams through branches, cinematic mist.
castle: Massive medieval fortress, stone walls, high towers, banners fluttering in the wind.
desert: Endless golden dunes, harsh sunlight, heat haze, lonely horizon.
city: Tall skyscrapers, street lights, neon signs, wet asphalt reflections at night.
village: Small rustic houses, cobblestone streets, warm lantern light.
ocean: Deep blue waves, foamy surf, sunlight reflecting off the water.
mountain: Jagged cliffs, snowy peaks, swirling clouds, grand scale.
space: Infinite black void, distant stars, glowing nebulae, cinematic lighting.
laboratory: Futuristic room with glass walls, glowing screens, sterile lighting.

-------------------------
CHARACTERS
-------------------------
detective: Trench coat, fedora hat, cigarette smoke, dark lighting, intense expression.
scientist: White lab coat, glasses, surrounded by monitors and holographic displays.
soldier: Tactical armor, weapon in hand, dusty environment, battlefield chaos.
astronaut: Reflective helmet, glowing visor, cosmic background.
child: Innocent face, simple clothing, wide eyes, natural lighting.
robot: Metallic humanoid, glowing eyes, sleek mechanical joints.
villain: Shadowy figure, dark clothing, menacing aura.
hero: Confident posture, determined gaze, cinematic backlight.
ghost: Translucent form, ethereal glow, cold color tone.

-------------------------
LIGHTING & MOOD
-------------------------
noir: Low key shadows, smoke-filled room, reflective puddles, monochrome palette.
sunset: Warm orange glow, long shadows, dramatic sky.
rain: Wet surfaces, reflections, falling droplets, blue-gray tone.
fire: Warm flickering light, glowing embers, dramatic contrast.
fog: Diffused light, low visibility, mysterious atmosphere.
snow: White ground, visible breath, cold lighting, soft tones.
destruction: Smoke, debris, broken structures, orange glow from fires.

-------------------------
OBJECTS & THEMES
-------------------------
weapon: Metallic glint, precision detail, dramatic close-up.
car: Shiny body, motion blur, headlights cutting through rain.
mirror: Reflected perspective, dual meaning, cinematic framing.
door: Half-open with light spilling through, symbolic transition.
computer: Futuristic UI glowing, reflections on user’s face.
bridge: Long structure over water or city, height and depth visible.
fireplace: Warm orange light illuminating a dark room.
clock: Symbol of urgency, close-up focus, ticking tension.

-------------------------
CINEMATIC QUALITIES
-------------------------
Use soft rim lighting for drama.
Include depth and atmosphere (fog, haze, dust).
Emphasize composition — foreground subject, clear background contrast.
Add cinematic color grading (teal & orange for action, cool tones for mystery, warm tones for drama).
